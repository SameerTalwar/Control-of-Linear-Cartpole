Autodesk Inventor

Export your Autodesk® Inventor® assembly models in a format compatible with Simscape™ Multibody™ software

Enable the Simscape Multibody Link plug-in on your Autodesk Inventor installation to begin exporting your CAD assemblies. Get started with a robotic arm export example or read about the assembly constraints that you can export and the Simscape Multibody joint and constraint blocks that they map into. You must have the Simscape Multibody Link plug-in installed before continuing.

Functions

smlink_linkinvAdd Simscape Multibody Link to your Windows registry as an Autodesk Inventor plugin
smlink_unlinkinvRemove Simscape Multibody Link from your Windows registry as an Autodesk Inventor plugin
